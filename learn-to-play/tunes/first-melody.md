# First Melody — "Pedestal Walk"

A 16-bar minor-pentatonic walk. Notation uses drum numbers 1-5; each note is a
single mallet strike at the drum centre. Tempo: 96 BPM, 4/4 time.

```
| 1 1 2 3 | 5 3 2 1 | 2 3 4 5 | 4 3 2 - |
| 1 3 5 3 | 2 4 5 4 | 3 2 1 - | 1 - - - |
| 5 4 3 2 | 1 2 3 4 | 5 5 4 3 | 2 1 - - |
| 3 5 4 3 | 2 4 3 2 | 1 3 2 1 | 1 - - - |
```

`-` = rest (single beat). Repeat for variations: try a snare-style buzz roll on
beats 2 and 4, or strike off-centre on drum-5 for a splash texture in the
second half.
