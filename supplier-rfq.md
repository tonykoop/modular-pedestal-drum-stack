# Supplier RFQ — Modular Pedestal Drum Stack

Quote-ready text to send to material and hardware suppliers.

---

## Steel suppliers (e.g. Online Metals, Metals Depot, Discount Steel)

Subject: Quote request — sheet and bar stock for hobby percussion instrument build

Hello,

I'm building a small custom percussion instrument and would like a quote on the
following stock. All quantities are for a single-instrument build with 10%
overage allowance.

1. **Cold-rolled steel sheet, 1.2 mm (18 ga)** — one 24" × 48" sheet
   (1018 or equivalent C1008 grade). Cut-to-size service welcome if you can
   pre-cut it to fit a 1500 mm × 700 mm working envelope on my plasma table.

2. **Cold-rolled steel plate, 6 mm (1/4 in)** — one 16" × 16" piece, 1018 grade.
   Plasma-cut tolerance acceptable on the perimeter; I'll finish-cut the disc
   profile in-house.

3. **Cold-rolled steel flat bar, 3 mm × 18 mm** — five 1 m lengths, 1018 grade.

Please quote with:
- Per-item price and total
- Lead time
- Shipping cost to [destination]
- Cut-to-size or shear charges if any

Thanks!

## Drum-parts supplier (Drum Supply House, Pearl direct, or equivalent)

Subject: Quote — modular drum-stack lug + rod + claw kit

Hello,

For a custom 5-drum instrument build (drum diameters 100/150/200/250/300 mm),
quoting:

- Pearl-style snare lug, die-cast zinc, standard M6 thread — **30 each**
- Tension rod, M6 fine thread × 50 mm length — **30 each**
- Tension rod nylon washer, M6 × 3 mm — **30 each**
- Claw hook, bent-steel flat 1.5 mm, fits 3 mm counter-hoop — **30 each**

I'll counter-hoop in-house from rolled 3 mm flat bar. Need the lugs and claws
to be compatible with a non-standard counter-hoop OD; please confirm the claw
profile clears a 3 mm hoop.

Thanks!
