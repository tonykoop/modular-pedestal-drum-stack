Sheet-metal greenfield blueprint · Round 5 · Lane 08Modular Pedestal Drum Stack

Five single-headed sheet-metal drums stacked on a central pedestal pole — pentatonic A-minor (A2-C3-E3-G3-A3), played top-down with mallets. A floor tom's footprint, a tom-stack's melodic range.

Family
Membranophone5 single-headed cylindrical drums
Shells
1.2 mm CRSRolled, TIG-seam, lathe-chamfered
Footprint
380 mm × 1450 mmBase plate × total height
Weight
~14 kgBase 5 · pole 1.5 · drums 5×1.4 · hw 0.5
Pitches
A2 C3 E3 G3 A3A-minor pentatonic (D omitted)
